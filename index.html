Who We Are

Commenced in 1999 with two hands working from home, established ourselves through hard work, devotion and dedication! Today we are working nation wide on various projects including School and College Campus, Hospitals, Commercial and Residential complexes, Townships, Industries, Water retaining structures, Swimming pools, STP, ETP etc. Arun Uttarwar & Associates is committed to meeting our client’s need by providing creative and cost efficient solutions. Empowering our team of qualified design engineers with Finite Element Analysis and Design software's like MIDAS, STAAD Pro, Struds and involvement of Mr. Arun Uttarwar in every project, has made it possible for us to deliver innovative and design safe structures

Our Promise
